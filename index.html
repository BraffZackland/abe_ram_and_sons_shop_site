                Consulting

                Instrument Appraisal & Inspection

                Buying, selling, insuring, or just curious what you have? A professional inspection gives you an accurate picture of an instrument's condition and value — before money changes hands or a claim gets filed.

                • Pre-purchase inspection — know what you're buying and what it will cost to bring to playing condition
                • Estate and collection appraisal — written valuation for insurance, sale, or probate
                • Condition reports for buyers, sellers, and auction consignments
                • Specialty focus on woodwinds, double reeds, and vintage instruments

                Remote inspection available for instruments that can be shipped. Written reports provided for all formal appraisals.
